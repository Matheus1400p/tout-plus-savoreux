About Us

Welcome to Tout Plus Savoureux! Since its opening in 1998, this charming French restaurant located in the heart of Chicago has become a reference in the city's gastronomy. Founded by Pierre Dubois, a renowned French chef who decided to change his life and bring his passion for French cuisine to the United States.

Located in a historic building in downtown Chicago, Tout Plus Savoureux combines Parisian elegance with the vibrant atmosphere of the city. The interior of the restaurant is tastefully decorated, offering a warm and refined atmosphere to its customers. Chef Pierre Dubois is known for his exceptional skill in French cuisine. Combining traditional techniques with contemporary innovations, he creates dishes that are true gastronomic works of art. Tout Plus Savoureux's menu is a celebration of classic French flavors, using fresh and seasonal ingredients to ensure a memorable culinary experience.The restaurant takes pride in its carefully curated wine list, featuring a variety of French and international labels. The sommelier is always available to guide customers in choosing the perfect pairing for their dishes. Since its arrival in Chicago, Tout Plus Savoureux has garnered achievements and recognition. The restaurant has received several awards, including Michelin stars and accolades in gastronomy publications, solidifying its reputation as one of the top destinations for refined culinary experiences in the city.

With its sophisticated ambiance and impeccable service, Tout Plus Savoureux is the ideal venue for special celebrations, romantic encounters, and memorable moments. Guests are greeted by a attentive staff dedicated to providing an exceptional gastronomic experience.
